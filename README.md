This is a game called S.S. Origins.
No the S.S. does not stand for the paramilitary organization and bodyguards of Nazi Germany, the Schutzstaffel.
The S.S. stands for Shadow and Silent, the greatest superhero duo to ever do it.
Except, they have not actually "done" it yet, because this is their origin story.
Their 4th or 5th one to be exact!
